Applies only to activities occurring on or after May 1, 2024

The assistant, calculator, category matcher, evidence guidance, folder tools, and ELMS-preparation tools use the current PGS rule era. Activities completed before May 1, 2024 must be reviewed under the guide and maximums that applied at that time.

Designed around teachers—not spreadsheets
