# Official Source Notes

## Primary source files used

- `9.1.23 PGS Reference Guide.pdf` - effective September 1, 2023; official category values, limits, contract-time rules, Title I exceptions, 225-CU requirement, and evidence expectations.
- `Documentation Required for CU Submission in ELMS.pdf` - category-specific documentation and current/expired category notes.
- `CCSD and CCEA PGS Announcements.pdf` - dated updates and special considerations, including LinkedIn Learning and Mental Health Academy.
- `Submitting Salary Advancement Documentation in CCSD ELMS.pdf` - submission workflow guidance.
- `Uploading and Sharing Salary Advancement Documentation Using Google Drive.pdf` - individual-file sharing guidance.
- `CU Approval Form - time based activities.pdf` - official Time-Based approval form.
- `CU Approval Form - university student assignment.pdf` - official University Student Assignment form.
- `CU Approval Form - Lower level College Coursework.pdf` - official Lower-Level College Coursework form.

## Critical rules surfaced in the app

- Column movement follows successful accrual of 225 CUs.
- Unless the guide gives an exception, only time outside the regular contractual workday may accrue CUs.
- Contract-rate hourly or extended-day/year time and Site-Based Collaboration Time are excluded.
- A paid supplemental-rate/stipend activity generally earns one CU per six hours; unpaid eligible time generally earns one CU per three hours.
- IEP/MDT activities have specific contract-time exceptions.
- Certain Title I PLC, tutoring, and mentoring activities may be paid outside the contractual day and still receive full CUs.
- Documentation for an activity/category must be combined into one final file and shared through an individual file link.
- Official guidance and dated announcements must be checked before final submission.

## Special announcements represented

- LinkedIn Learning activities completed on or after February 15, 2024: CTE teachers only through the announced CTE process.
- Self-designed graduate-credit courses after September 13, 2022: not accepted under the announcement.
- Identified Idaho State University COVID-19 transcript-credit process after May 27, 2021: not accepted.
- Identified Southern Utah University EDPD 5000-5999 credits after April 14, 2023: not accepted when based only on prior CCSD professional-learning certificates without additional university instruction.
- Mental Health Academy guidance beginning March 12, 2026 and the March 30, 2026 approved/not-approved course list announcement.
