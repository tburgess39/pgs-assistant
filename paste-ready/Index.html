Unofficial Guide
The FamilyPD PGS Assistant is an educator-created planning and documentation tool. It is not an official CCSD, CCEA, PGS, or ELMS application. CCSD makes every final eligibility, documentation, calculation, and approval decision.

Generated Approval-Form Packets

For supported categories, the assistant groups eligible entries from the same category, separates incompatible paid/unpaid calculation groups, and creates a Google Doc and PDF draft.

  • Time-Based form: maximum 20 activity records per page.
  • University Student Assignment form: maximum 5 assignments per page.
  • Lower-Level College Coursework form: maximum 5 courses per page.
  • Extra entries automatically continue onto additional pages.
  • Signed or final packets are not overwritten or duplicated in later packets.
Generated forms are assistant-prepared drafts. Review every name, date, time, description, payment designation, total, and category before signing or submitting. Use the current official form when CCSD requires the original form.

What Is Saved Where?

Google SheetActivity entries, calculations, statuses, profile information, and generated-packet records.
Google Drive foldersEvidence files and generated Google Doc/PDF drafts. Evidence is not uploaded automatically.
ELMSThe assistant does not submit to ELMS. The teacher completes the official ELMS submission.

Protect the Connection to Your Workspace

Do not restructure assistant-managed files.
The assistant relies on stable Google Sheet tabs, column headers, technical fields, folder IDs, and numbered folder names. Changing them can break calculations, disconnect saved links, cause duplicate folders, or prevent generated documents from being filed correctly.
Do not changeSheet tab names, column/field names, hidden tabs, technical columns, managed folder locations, or numbered subfolder names.
Safe actionsView/filter the Activity Log, open links, upload evidence files, open generated documents, and download or print copies.
Need a correction?Return to the website and edit the activity there instead of changing the Google Sheet directly.

Privacy and Student Information

Do not enter student names, student ID numbers, parent names, signatures, or other protected information into this assistant. IEP/MDT signature pages must be fully redacted before they are uploaded as evidence.

Before You Submit

  • Verify the category against current official PGS resources and announcements.
  • Confirm the evidence requirements and category maximum.
  • Review the CU total against the official ELMS record.
  • Share the final evidence file so authorized CCSD users with the link can view it.
  • Submit the final file link, not the category-folder link.
